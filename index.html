What We'll Do Together

From ideation to execution, here's how we build, learn, and grow as a team.

01

Build Real-World Projects

Work on actual products and tools that solve real problems. From web apps to cybersecurity tools, mobile applications to AI systems—we build things that people actually use. You'll gain hands-on experience with modern frameworks, best practices, and collaborative development workflows.

02

Weekly Tech Workshops

Every week, we dive deep into different technologies. Learn web development (React, Next.js), backend systems (Node.js, Python), cybersecurity fundamentals, cloud computing, and more. These aren't just lectures—they're interactive sessions where you code alongside experienced developers.

03

Hackathons & Competitions

Participate in local and national hackathons as a team. We organize our own events and compete in major competitions. Build projects in 24-48 hours, pitch to judges, win prizes, and add impressive accomplishments to your portfolio.

04

Industry Connections

Meet engineers from tech companies, startup founders, and cybersecurity professionals. Through speaker events, mentorship programs, and networking sessions, you'll build relationships that can shape your career path and open doors to internships and opportunities.

05

Community Initiatives

Use technology for good. Work on projects for local nonprofits, teach coding to younger students, organize tech literacy events, and create solutions that benefit the Tri-Valley community. Make a tangible difference while building your skills.

06

Leadership & Growth

Take on leadership roles, manage projects, coordinate events, and help shape the direction of the chapter. Develop soft skills like communication, project management, and team coordination—capabilities that are just as valuable as coding skills.

Why Students Really Join

Here's what makes TVT Pleasanton different from everything else.

🚀

It's Not Just Another Club

This isn't about showing up to meetings and calling it a day. TVT is a high-agency environment where you actually build things, ship products, and create work you're proud to share. If you want to go beyond surface-level learning and create something real, this is where you belong.

💡

Learn Skills That Actually Matter

Stop watching tutorials that go nowhere. Here, you'll learn by doing—building real projects with modern tools and frameworks. You'll understand how professional developers work, contribute to actual codebases, and develop skills that translate directly to internships and jobs.

🤝

Work With Driven People

Surround yourself with motivated, talented students who actually care about building things. The people you work with will push you to level up, collaborate on ambitious projects, and become friends who share your interests and drive.

📈

Build an Impressive Portfolio

Create projects that stand out on college applications, resumes, and LinkedIn. Show real impact, measurable results, and technical competence. When you apply to internships or colleges, you'll have concrete evidence of what you can do.

🎯

Cybersecurity Focus

Beyond general software development, we dive deep into cybersecurity—one of the fastest-growing and most in-demand fields. Learn penetration testing, secure coding practices, vulnerability research, and defensive security alongside experienced mentors in the field.

⚡

Actually Enjoy What You're Doing

Work on projects you find genuinely interesting. Have autonomy over what you build. Experiment with cutting-edge technologies. Make mistakes, learn fast, and iterate. This is where curiosity meets execution, and where your ideas can actually come to life.

The Bottom Line

If you're serious about technology, want to build real skills, work on meaningful projects, and be part of a community that values execution over talk—TVT Pleasanton is the place. We're looking for students who are curious, motivated, and ready to do the work. No experience required, just the willingness to learn and build.
